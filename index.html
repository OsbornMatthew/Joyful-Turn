WHY JOYFUL TURN

A Generation Is Turning Away.
We Believe It Can Turn Back.

The Burden

We're living in the last days, and so many young people are drifting from God — distracted, discouraged, or simply unaware of how near He still is.

The Turn

Joyful Turn is a fellowship built for this moment — gathering youth to worship, study the Word, and find their way back to a real relationship with Christ.

The Joy

This isn't a turn back to rules — it's a turn back to joy. To the relationship our souls were made for, restored and made new in Him.

Our Mission

To inspire, encourage, and disciple young people toward a strong, lasting relationship with Jesus Christ — equipping a generation to live according to His purpose in these last days.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Osborn Matthew

Hi, I'm Osborn Matthew
Founder of Joyful Turn.

I was born and raised in Tiruchirappalli in a pastor's family and have been blessed to know Christ from a young age. God called me into ministry in 2020 and gave me a vision to help turn the hearts of young people back to Jesus Christ.

Through Joyful Turn, my mission is to inspire, encourage, and disciple youth to build a strong relationship with God and live according to His purpose. I hold a B.Tech in Information Technology and continue serving God with a passion to see lives transformed by His love and truth.
